App Won't Start or Crashes

Step 1: Restart your device completely
Step 2: Check if you have enough storage space (at least 100MB free)
Step 3: Update Dyed Rabbit to the latest version from your app store
Step 4: Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall the app (iOS)

Painting/Drawing Issues

Touch not responding: Clean your screen and ensure no screen protector interference
Brush not working: Try switching brush types or adjusting brush size
Colors not appearing: Check if you've selected a color from the palette

Saving & Gallery Issues

Can't save artwork: Check storage permissions in device settings
Images not in gallery: Look for "Dyed Rabbit" folder in your photos app
Poor image quality: Ensure you have sufficient device storage and memory

‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Dyed Rabbit free to use?
A: Yes! Dyed Rabbit is completely free with all features unlocked. No hidden costs or in-app purchases.
Q: Can I use Dyed Rabbit offline?
A: Absolutely! Once downloaded, Dyed Rabbit works completely offline. No internet connection required for coloring.
Q: How do I backup my artwork?
A: Your artwork is automatically saved to your device's gallery. You can also use cloud backup services to sync your photos.
Q: Can I add my own pictures to color?
A: Currently, Dyed Rabbit comes with curated coloring templates. Custom image import is planned for future updates.
Q: Why can't I see the preview button for some images?
A: Preview buttons only appear for images that have colored reference versions available for comparison.
